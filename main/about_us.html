SalesVista was established in 2017

SalesVista was established to solve a common problem that organizations across the world face how to calculate complex sales commissions and give clarity and business insight both to sales and finance professionals into overall sales team performance.

Gone are the days of managing complex commission payouts over email threads and spreadsheets. With SalesVista, you can reduce the amount of time you spend managing variable compensation, without sacrificing quality. We’ve defined the process, so it’s easier to understand and we’ve streamlined the process, so it’s easier to execute.
